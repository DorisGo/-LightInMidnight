## Features
✨ Spatial Canvas
Visualize life traces as geometric symbols instead of traditional lists.

🕰 Timeline
Browse your memories by week, month, or year.

🎨 Shape Preferences
Customize how different trace types appear on the canvas.

💾 Persistent Storage
Automatically save and restore traces using LocalStorage.

📝 Complete Trace Lifecycle
Create, edit, view, and delete every trace.

## Philosophy

Your traces are not achievements.

They are simply moments that became part of your life.

## Why Light In Midnight?

Most apps encourage us to quantify life.

Reading streaks.

Movie counts.

Daily check-ins.

Light In Midnight chooses another way.

It isn't about productivity.

It is about quietly leaving traces of the books, movies, music, places, and moments that became part of our lives.

Because recording makes us feel our existence.
